### Categorizing AI Coding Assistants
- **AI Architect**: Chat interfaces powered by top-tier LLMs used primarily for high-level logic and strategy. They are best for planning complex systems, generating large chunks of boilerplate code, and helping you understand difficult concepts.
- **AI Copilot**: Lightweight plugins added to traditional code editors that use smaller, faster models to assist during active manual coding. They excel at providing real-time tab completions and quick inline chat fixes.
- **AI Workshop**: Comprehensive IDEs built for AI integration (such as Cursor or Zed) that possess full codebase awareness. By combining the reasoning of an Architect with the proximity of a Copilot, they are the gold standard for agentic work, such as multi-file refactoring, deep debugging, and performing complex edits across your entire project simultaneously.
